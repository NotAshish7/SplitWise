Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

1.Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account data: name, email address, and password (stored hashed)
  • Profile data: preferred currency, profile picture
  • Usage data: expenses, groups, payments you create
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type for security purposes
2.How We Use Your Data
  • To provide, operate, and maintain SplitWise services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g. email delivery, OAuth providers like Google/Facebook) strictly to operate the service. These providers are contractually obligated to keep your data secure.

SplitWise uses browser localStorage to store your authentication token and user preferences (e.g. preferred currency). We do not use third-party tracking cookies. You can clear this data at any time by logging out or clearing browser storage.

5.Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account via Settings, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days, except where required by law.

6.Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures: passwords are hashed with bcrypt, API communication uses HTTPS, JWTs expire after 7 days, and all database access requires authentication. However, no system is 100% secure — please use a strong, unique password.

7.Your Rights
  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
